Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?

24 hours from when you submit your intake form. We send you a preview link via email.

What if I need changes after you build it?

That's what your monthly updates are for! Starter gets 1/month, Pro gets 4/month, Enterprise gets unlimited. Just email us what you want changed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime and you won't be billed the following month.

What if I don't like my website?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not happy, we'll refund your setup fee. No questions asked.

Do you build e-commerce sites?

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include e-commerce functionality. You can sell products directly from your site.

What about domain and hosting?

Hosting is included in your monthly fee. If you don't have a domain, we'll help you register one (~$15/year).
